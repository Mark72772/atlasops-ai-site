What proof covers

What proof prevents

No unsupported send, post, revenue, or delivery claims. External actions require policy approval and a proof record.

Use proof before claiming complete

Every AtlasOps audit, offer, outreach draft, and implementation path should link back to a test, route check, artifact, approval, or payment proof where applicable.
