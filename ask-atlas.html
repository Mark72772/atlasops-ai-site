FAQ

What is the AI Business Automation Audit?

A practical review of one business workflow, website/payment CTA, follow-up path, and automation opportunity map.

What do I receive?

A workflow map, opportunity scorecard, risk notes, suggested tools, and a short roadmap for the next useful automation step.

What should I send?

A public website link, the workflow you want reviewed, current tools, and where follow-up or handoff breaks down.

How does payment work?

Payment is through PayPal.me. AtlasOps verifies payment locally from evidence before delivery.

Can Atlas answer questions before I pay?

Yes. Ask Atlas can answer basic service-fit questions when the relay or email fallback is connected.

Can Atlas access my systems?

Not through the public form. Deeper technical review requires explicit authorization and scope.

Is this live AI chat?

Ask Atlas is live only when the Cloudflare Worker reply loop and local Atlas polling are configured and verified. Otherwise it falls back to email.

What should I not send through the public form?

Do not send passwords, API keys, payment card data, private files, or sensitive customer data.

How fast will AtlasOps reply?

If the relay is online, keep the page open for a near-live reply. If not, AtlasOps follows up by email after local sync.

What happens after I submit?

The relay stores the question, local Atlas pulls it, creates a local question/lead record, drafts a governed reply, and sends only after policy allows it.
