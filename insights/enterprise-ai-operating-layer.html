What happened

OpenAI framed enterprise AI as a move toward a unified operating layer where agents are grounded in business context, connected to systems, governed by permissions, and improved through evaluation loops.

Why it matters for business owners

Point tools can create more handoff work. The useful move is a workflow map that shows which agent should act, what data it can touch, and where approval is required.

Practical workflow action

  1. Choose one workflow.
  2. Define the owner.
  3. Map systems of record.
  4. Define permissions.
  5. Define the approval gate.
  6. Measure the outcome.
  7. Then automate.
