AI SEO is becoming a business visibility problem

Your website may rank on Google and still be hard for answer engines to explain. The fix starts with clarity, proof, and a clear next action.

Why service businesses should automate one workflow first

AI is becoming useful inside real workflows, but a service business should start with one process: lead intake, estimate follow-up, service routing, reminders, or website inquiry response.

Takeaway: Pick one workflow, one owner, one approval gate, and one measurable outcome before buying more AI tools.
