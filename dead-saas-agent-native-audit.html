Public-source first, authorized deeper review only

Public-source scouting uses public data only: public websites, public marketplaces, public changelogs, public reviews, public app listings, public social pages, and public product documents when accessible. AtlasOps does not bypass logins, paywalls, or access controls.

Deeper product review, code review, support-ticket analysis, analytics review, customer-data processing, or old customer email use requires documented authorization and privacy review first.

Customer data and email lists require legal/privacy review before processing or audience use. AtlasOps does not upload customer lists to ad platforms without documented rights, security review, and explicit approval.

No guaranteed MRR, acquisition outcome, valuation, ad result, customer response, or relaunch revenue is promised. Ad spend is never launched without approval.

Process

  • Start with a public-source candidate scan or a founder-provided product.
  • Score workflow clarity, prior traction signals, inactivity signals, privacy risk, and agent-native fit.
  • Create a diligence checklist and data-rights review before any private material is imported.
  • Map the old dashboard workflow into one agent-native MVP with human approval points.
  • Draft a relaunch landing page, content plan, UTM links, and ad copy for review.
